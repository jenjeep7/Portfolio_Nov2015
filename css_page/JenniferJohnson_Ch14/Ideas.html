Don't be Afraid of Color!

One of the biggest trends right now is mixing textures and colors. Don't be afraid to mix color into places unexpected! Kitchen islands are a great way to bring in color without having to redo everything. Painting just the island really makes it stand out and brings unexpected character. Feeling really adventurous? If you are picking out new kitchen cabinets, you may want to consider something different than the standard stains. Ask your contractor about some of the new finishes available. Painted and distressed finishes can really warm up a new kitchen and make it feel like home.

Spring is Here!

Spring is here and it is time to get back outdoors! One of the first things on your Spring to-do list should be sprucing up your yard. Here are some tips to getting that lush green grass you desire!

  • Clean up winter debris
  • Clean up after pets
  • Fertilize and put down weed preventer

We highly recommend hiring a professional to fertilize and treat your lawn. It is an affordable carefree way to ensure your yard is getting exactly what it needs.

Trends

Kitchens

Painted cabinets and the use of distressing techniques to give a warm feeling are hot right now.

Bathrooms

High-end finishes are still popular in today's bathrooms. Upgraded fixtures, tile and luxurious tubs are still big in bathroom trends.

Basements

Customized spaces are huge right now. Home theatres, workout areas and even areas decidated to pets are popular. The basement is an area that people can get creative!

Backyards

Today's backyard has become an extention of the home's livng area. Backyard kitchens, eating areas and even living areas are hugely popular.
